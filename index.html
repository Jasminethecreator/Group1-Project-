Lyndsey Scott

Modeling

After finding confidence in her new self-image during college and with the encouragement from her friends, Lyndsey decided to pursue modeling. Searching for model work was not easy, for two years every agency she approached turned her down. Lyndsey’s parents even started to get discourage and tried to convince her to go back to Computer Science. However, in 2008 things started to slowly pick-up and she was contracted by Click Model Management of New York City. Unfortunately, “Click” did not see Lyndsey’s full potential as a model because she was considered too old at the age of 24 so they recommended that she say she was 5 years younger. During this residency, Lyndsey was featured in videos for Elle Girl. In 2009, while passing out flyers for a side job, she got the infamous call from fashion house Calvin Klein, becoming the first black model to sign an exclusive runway contract with them. This moment in her modeling career created a domino effect. Up into the year of 2010, other prestigious fashion houses such as Prada, Gucci, Baby Phat, Fendi, and several others called on Lyndsey to walk their runways. She also changed agencies to Elite Model Management. In 2013 she was answering questions on Quora about her life experiences she attention to her programming skills.

Acting

Scott’s acting came as no surprise with her background and education is the theater arts. Signed with Luber Roklin Management in Los Angeles she has a varied resume from film, television and of course theater. She has many short film credits best known as “Jackie” in Coffee Snobs and “Becca” in Saddles. It is known that Lyndsey is now working on a screenplay on her story as a model/actress and engineer. It was last known to be in preproduction as of September 2019.

Coding

  • Started coding at 12 on her calculator.
  • Graduated from Amherst College majoring in Comp Sci and Theater.
  • Currently takes on freelance iOS Software Engineering along side her acting career.
  • Has built apps for:
    • Susan G. Komen Foundation
    • AIDS Walk
    • Cystic Fibrosis Foundation
  • Lead iOS Engineer at Rallybound.
  • Ranked in the top 2% of question answered on Stack Overflow.
  • A member of the Ray wenderlich.com iOS tutorial team.
  • Created video tutorials for kids w/ costar such as Bill Gates and Mark Zuckerberg for Code.org.
  • Featured on CNN, The BBC, NPR, Good Morning America, Forbes, Harper’s Bazaar, and many others for her computer programming skills and efforts to promote diversity in tech.
  • Named one of the 100 Women of the Year for both UK Elle and AskMen.com.
  • Received the a “Lifetime of Inspiration” award from Harvard University’s Women in Business organization.
